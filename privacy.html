Privacy Policy — NATTLJUS

Last updated: 5 July 2026

NATTLJUS does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data we collect

None. NATTLJUS has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no trackers. We do not ask for your name, email, location, contacts, or any other personal information.

Network access

The app makes no network connections of any kind. It works fully offline — you can play it in Airplane Mode. The daily puzzle is computed on your device from the date; nothing is downloaded or uploaded.

On-device data

Your level progress, moth stamps, collected album motifs, daily-puzzle streak, and sound and haptics settings are saved locally on your device using the system's standard UserDefaults storage. These values never leave your device and are not accessible to us or anyone else. Deleting the app removes them.

Third parties

NATTLJUS contains no third-party SDKs, ad networks, or analytics services.

Children

The app is made for all ages (4+) and collects no data from anyone, including children. There are no ads, no purchases, and no links out of the game.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
