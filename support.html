NATTLJUS — Support

One spark — a garden of light. A wordless card puzzle for iPhone and iPad.

How to play

Every card carries firefly dots — that many neighbors must be glowing before the card wakes up. Tap a card and Lily the luna moth delivers a spark; light then cascades from card to card. Wake the whole garden! Koi cards wake their twin anywhere on the board, Star Prisms count as two neighbors, and Brambles can only be woken by the cascade, never tapped.

You can never lose: drag the firefly slider to rewind the light, step by step. Solving a garden with fewer sparks earns more moth stamps. If you are stuck for a while, Lily flies to a helpful card and hovers there.

FAQ

Where is my progress stored? Locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded — the game is fully offline.

What is the Full Moon puzzle? One puzzle per day, computed from the date (UTC) — identical for every player worldwide. Finished days glow as moons in your streak.

How do I unlock the next garden? Collect moth stamps. Each garden opens at the stamp count shown on its card.

What are Gilded cards? Solve a level at par (or better) and its motifs are minted in gold foil in the Living Album.

No sound? Check the speaker toggle on the title screen and your device's silent switch — NATTLJUS respects it.

Which languages? The game itself is wordless; menus and the tutorial speak English, German, and Swedish automatically.
